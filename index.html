• ABOUT ME •

Hello, and welcome to DYO Studio! I'm Yvonne Opulencia, a college student with a passion for art. As an artist, I explore a variety of mediums, from traditional pencil portraits to colored pencil drawings to digital art and murals. My creative journey is inspired by my cousin and aunt. This portfolio is where I share my artistic growth and my unique perspective. Join me on this exciting adventure in the world of art!
